Self-Care Tools & Professional Access


Self-Care Techniques

Meditation

Relax your mind with these steps:


  • Find a quiet place
  • Sit comfortably
  • Focus on your breath
  • Practice for 5-10 minutes

Breathing Exercises

Improve focus with these steps:


  • Sit comfortably
  • Inhale deeply for 4 seconds
  • Hold your breath for 7 seconds
  • Exhale slowly for 8 seconds

Gratitude Journaling

Start journaling with these ideas:


  • Write down 3 things you’re grateful for
  • Describe one positive experience
  • Reflect on your accomplishments

Exercise

Boost your physical and mental well-being with these activities:


  • Go for a 30-minute walk
  • Stretch for 10 minutes
  • Try a short workout or yoga session
  • Dance to your favorite music

Digital Detox

Unplug and recharge with these steps:


  • Set your phone on silent or airplane mode
  • Avoid screens for one hour
  • Spend time outdoors or with loved ones
  • Read a physical book or write in a journal

Creative Expression

Engage your creativity with these activities:


  • Draw or doodle for 10 minutes
  • Write a short poem or story
  • Create a playlist of your favorite songs
  • Cook a new recipe or bake something
